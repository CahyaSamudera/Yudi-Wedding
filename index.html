Protokol Kesehatan

Kami menyadari jika di tengah kondisi pandemi ini, kesehatan adalah yang paling utama. Karena itu, kami menyampaikan beberapa catatan berikut:
  1. Jika Bapak / Ibu / Sdr berkenan hadir, kami sangat mengapresiasinya. Mohon hadir dengan memakai masker medis/kain berlapis serta jaga jarak di lokasi dan menghindari kerumunan.

  2. Jika Bapak / Ibu / Sdr memilih untuk tidak hadir secara langsung, kamipun juga menghargainya. Kami menyediakan tautan Zoom / Streaming Via YouTube.

Health Protocol

We are fully aware that our health is on top priority, especially in this pandemic situation. Thus, we'd like to share the following notes:
  1. If you decided to attend in person, we'd feel very honoured and we really appreciate it. Please remember to wear a medical or cloth face mask and apply physical distancing along the event.

  2. We also understand if you decided to attend virtually. We will provide you a Zoom Meeting / YouTube Live for the event.
